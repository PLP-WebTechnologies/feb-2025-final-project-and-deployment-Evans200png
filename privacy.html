Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShopSmart! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address, and payment details.
  • Account Information: Login credentials and profile data when you register an account.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, time spent on site, and actions taken.
  • Device Data: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process orders and manage your account
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (you may opt-out at any time)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure site security

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Track shopping cart items
  • Analyze site traffic and performance
  • Deliver personalized content and ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your shopping experience.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your data with:

  • Trusted payment processors (e.g., PayPal, Stripe)
  • Shipping and logistics partners (for order delivery)
  • Analytics and marketing tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as necessary for order fulfillment, legal compliance, and business operations. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing emails

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@shopsmart.com.

8. Children's Privacy

ShopSmart does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
